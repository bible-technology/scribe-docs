- Click on the **bell** icon <img src="/autographaAssetsV2/how-to/notification.png" width="30px" alt=""/> at the top right side of the screen (next to the **New column** icon.
  - Here you will find a list of app notifications. 	
  - Each type of notification will have a different color. 

To return to the main page, click on the <—— arrow on the top left corner of the screen.
