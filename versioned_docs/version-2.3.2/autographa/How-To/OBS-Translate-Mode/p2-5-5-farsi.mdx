---

To make translating OBS projects from Farsi to other languages easier, Farsi numbers have been introduced.

**How to use Farsi numbers in OBS**

- Go to the profile icon on the top right side of the screen <img src="/autographaAssetsV2/how-to/farsi.png" width="30px" alt=""/>.
- Click on it and select " your profile".
- Go to the App language option and select Farsi language.
- Click on "Save" button.
- Now in OBS projects, you can see Farsi Numbers.
