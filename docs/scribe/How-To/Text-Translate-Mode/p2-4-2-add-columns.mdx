title: Adding New Columns
---

- Find the **Columns** icon <img src="/scribeAssetsV2/how-to/add-cloumn.png"  width="50px" alt="add column"/> on the top right corner of the screen. 
- Click on it to change the number of columns shown on the screen.
- **Scribe** supports up to three columns open at a time (including the editing column). 
- Each column can be further divided into two sections, and the user can simultaneously refer to four translation resources.
