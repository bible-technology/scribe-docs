To import a resource, follow the steps below.

-  Click on the blue, circular plus sign icon <img src="/autographaAssetsV2/how-to/import.png" width="30px" alt=""/> on the bottom-right corner of the screen. 
-  Enter the resource name.
-  Enter the door43 URL of your resource.
-  Click **Import** and it should show up in the listing.
